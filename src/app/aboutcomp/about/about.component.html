Know Us
Jaago Bharat primarily aims to bring the deplorable condition of Our Environment, Water bodies, Waste management and Education, in the forefront.

  • Around 660 metric tonnes of municipal waste is generated every day in Noida. It includes wet waste, paper waste , plastic waste, electronic waste and it goes on to include all other kinds of waste which are dumped together. Our team is working towards the solution of this waste problem, to provide some easy techniques and methods for the people .

  • Water level has been decreasing drastically and could lead to a crisis if not halted. We are approaching every society and apartments to insist them to install rainwater harvesting system.

  • To achieve our dream of being a developed nation education is the key as our great leader Mahatma Gandhi once said, “Education is the basic tool for the development of consciousness and the reconstitution of society.

  • Social media has now become the biggest part of our life, apart from Facebook, Twitter and Instagram page we also have our youtube channel named as “Jaago Bharat Front” and had made various short films on different issues like Ragging, Disability, Education, Women empowerment etc.

  • And, last but not the least, we need to realize that if Fundamental Rights is the Heart of a Democracy, Fundamental Duties are its Soul.

Jaago Bharat was established to bring in the forefront the issues which are relevant for any developed society/nation but are left out in oblivion.
